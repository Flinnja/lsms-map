Links and resources

Here we’ve curated a shortlist of resources with the aim of providing a streamlined directory of the support networks out there. Resources include helplines for depression, anxiety, Covid-19 related stressors, support for LGBTI+ young people, and Pasifika families.

All Right

A Canterbury-based organisation that offers tips to maintaining wellbeing, particularly in response to widespread disasters.

Depression Helpline

A national organisation with information about and guidance for dewaling with depression. Free call 0800 111 757 or text 4202 to talk to a trained counsellor.

Anxiety Helpline

0800 ANXIETY (0800 269 4389) provides free national 24/7 support, information, and advice about managing anxiety and stress, including taking callers through breathing and relaxation techniques, and supports callers to learn and practice anxiety management skills.

RainbowYOUTH

Provides free 1:1 peer support for LGBTI+ young people, their friends and whānau, to provide information and guidance about everything to do with gender, sexuality, and being queer and trans.

Le Va

Le Va's purpose is to support Pasifika families and communities to unleash their full potential and have the best possible health and wellbeing outcomes.
